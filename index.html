Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the game collects, uses, and shares information when you play it on the Poki platform.

Information We Collect

The game does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not ask for your name, email, address, phone number, or any other personal details.

Through the third-party analytics service GameAnalytics, we collect anonymous gameplay data, including:

  • Game session length and frequency
  • Gameplay events (level progression, in-game choices, deaths, upgrades)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • General location (country level, based on IP address — the IP itself is not stored)

This information is collected automatically and is fully anonymous. It cannot be used to identify individual players.

How We Use the Information

The data collected is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Understanding how players interact with the game
  • Improving game design, balance, and user experience
  • Identifying and fixing bugs or technical issues

The data is used for statistical analysis only and is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

The game uses the following third-party service:

GameAnalytics — A game analytics platform that collects anonymous gameplay data. You can review GameAnalytics' privacy practices at: https://gameanalytics.com/privacy/

The game is hosted on the Poki platform. Poki's own privacy policy applies as well and can be found at: https://poki.com/en/privacy

Children's Privacy

The game is suitable for general audiences, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All analytics data collected is anonymous and cannot be used to identify any individual player, including children.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained by GameAnalytics according to their data retention policy. No personal data is stored by the game itself.

Your Choices

By playing the game, you consent to the anonymous data collection described in this policy. If you do not wish to have your gameplay data collected, please discontinue playing the game.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
